# Credits

## Cannonballs! Resurrection — created by Kenneth

The entire **Cannonballs! Resurrection** project is the work of **Kenneth ("Ken")**. Everything that
makes this archive worth keeping — the website, the FAQ, the years of pestering WildTangent to bring
the game back, and especially the **"Access all Islands" patch** that removes the trial/registration
gate and unlocks every island — is his.

- 2016 follow-up as **CaptSmokey6** on the WildTangent Restoration blog (see docs/kenneth-2016-followup.md); current contact: **ken@captsmokey.com** / **captsmokey6@gmail.com**

In his own words, on why he did it: WildTangent discontinued *Cannonballs!* claiming low lobby
traffic and new-tech incompatibility; Kenneth countered that it ran fine on then-current Windows and
that the lobby regularly had 50+ players. He vowed to bring it back and release it as **freeware**,
and he built the all-islands patch and was working toward fully cracking the retail version.

**Thank you, Kenneth.** This archive exists so your project isn't lost.

A nod also to **Crossbones**, a community member from the original Cannonballs! lobby who's referenced
on Kenneth's links page.

## Original game
*Cannonballs!* (2002) was made and published by **WildTangent, Inc.** The IP is now held by
**Gamigo AG** (acquired WildTangent's catalog in 2019). This archive claims no ownership of the game.

## Dependencies referenced
- **Microsoft Java Virtual Machine** — © Microsoft. Required by the WildTangent Web Driver.

## Preservation context
- The **Internet Archive** `wildtangent-archive` collection and the community **pug.quest WildTangent
  Archive** (and its Discord) have preserved WildTangent titles, including Cannonballs.

## This archive
Compiled and documented in 2026 as a preservation effort, to credit Kenneth and record what it takes
to run the game on modern hardware. The macOS/VM scripts and the attempt log are the only original
contributions here; the project itself is Kenneth's.
