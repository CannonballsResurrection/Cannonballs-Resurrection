GETTING STARTED

So, you're a first-time player? Once you've reached the title screen, you should see the following options...

User Login
New User
Guest Login
Single Player
Leaderboards

User Login

Press this button if you have already created a new account. Every time you return to play the game, this is the button you should press to begin - it takes you to the Game Lobby.

New User

As a first time player, this is the option you should select. This allows you to create a new player ID for the game. It requires that you enter your email address for uniqueness - please note that your email address will not be distributed or sold. If you have questions about our privacy policy, you can read about it here.

Guest Login

If you'd just like to check out the game, you can play as a guest - this requires no registration - you can simply enter in a name and play. You will not, however, be able to access all the maps and weapons within the game, nor visit the Registered Users Room.

Single Player

If you would like to play against the computer by yourself, you can select to play Single Player mode.

Leaderboards

Viewing the Leaderboards allows you to check out the worldwide statistics for all players - you might show up in the top 10!

Tips:

  • For the best experience, you may want to play the game in fullscreen. You can switch in and out of fullscreen mode by clicking the minimize/maximize button in the top right.
  • If you experience poor performance, you may want to turn off shadows in the game - just pull down the options dropdown from the title bar, and turn shadows off.

Playing the Game

Object: The object of Cannonballs!TM is to destroy all the other players on the map. Players are destroyed by a direct hit by an offensive weapon, by drowning, or by being caught in the blast of a TNT barrel. Destroying another player also awards you all of their remaining gold.

Game Play: Gameplay in Cannonballs!TM is simple - use the arrow keys to tilt and rotate your cannon. Press the spacebar to fire the currently selected weapon. You can choose a different weapon by selecting it from the dropdown menu in the top-right of the screen. You may also choose a different camera view by selecting it from the camera drop down menu in the title bar. The shot cam is recommended for finding the best shot

During your turn, you may attack other players with any weapon you can afford (each weapon costs a specific amount of gold. Weapons that you cannot use are grayed out). Press the spacebar once to begin firing, and when the power bar reaches the desired force, press the spacebar again.

When it is not your turn (defense mode), you may use defensive weapons. Defensive weapons cannot directly destroy any other player - however, they help you to dodge other player's shots, or block them. Weapons like the molehill can also be used to help you zero in on your target.

Wind:  Watch out for the wind! The wind indicator in the bottom right of the screen shows you the direction the wind is blowing, and at what speed. Wind makes a big difference in your shots, and can drastically affect your aim!

TNT Barrels:   TNT barrels detonate when struck. Anything within the radius of their explosion detonates as well - including treasure chests, other TNT barrels, and players. Watch out! You can even detonate yourself. TNT barrels can be used to start chain-reactions of explosions, to collect lots of treasure at once, or to kill multiple players at the same time. They are also an effective way of blocking another player's shots.

Treasure Chests:   Treasure chests contain random amounts of gold - destroy treasure chests to collect gold and finance bigger and better weapons. Some treasure chests also contain an instant teleport!
