The Lobby

The lobby is where you'll meet other players and create or join games. It consists of 3 main panels -

  • Game Panel
     The Game Panel shows all games currently available to join. It is the largest panel on the screen, and appears in the top left. Games which are not full will have a join button next to them - click on this button to join the game!
  • User Panel
     The User Panel shows all players currently in this game room. Users with a crossed swords icon next to their names are currently in a game, and will not respond to chat. Users with a zzz next to their name are 'away'.
  • Chat Panel
     The Chat Panel is visible at the bottom of the page. It shows all ongoing chat. You can type your own message and post it by typing at the bottom of the screen and pressing enter.

Rooms

By default, you will be placed into the room with the most players in it. You can switch the room you are currently in at any time by clicking on the Room Dropdown menu just above the Game Panel. You will only see games, users, and chat for the room you are currently in.

Changing Your Status

At any time, you can change your user state by clicking on the I Am Awake/I Am Away dropdown and selecting the desired state. If you need to step away for a cup of coffee, change the user state to I Am Away and all other players will see a zzz icon next to your name, letting them know you are away.

Beginning A Game

To begin a new game, click on the Begin New Game button, and you will be prompted to create your game. You may select any one of the available maps for your game to be set on. Each has its own unique advantages and disadvantages. There are several options you can alter to customize your game experience - note that YOU DO NOT HAVE TO ALTER THESE SETTINGS! When you're ready to create the game, click CREATE.

  • Maximum # Of Players
    This is the number of players you will allow to enter the game. If you only want to play a 2 player game, set this number to '2'.
  • Starting # Of Lives
     This is the number of times players can be destroyed before they are out of the game.
  • Starting Gold
     This is the amount of gold every player starts with
  • HotSeat Mode
     In HotSeat mode, players only have a certain number of seconds per turn. By setting this, you can alter the speed of play
  • Treasures Respawn
     Treasures respawn by default - which means that every time a treasure chest is destroyed, a new one appears somewhere else. Turning this off SEVERELY limits the amount of money used in a game
  • Team Play
     Team Play allows players to join into teams - 2 against 2 is lots of fun!

Joining A Game

To join a game, look for one to appear in the Game Panel. Once the Join button appears, click it to join the game. You will be taken to the Join Menu, where you will see all other players in the game, and you will be asked to confirm your join and select a color for your cannon. Once the creator of the game elects to start, the game will begin!
